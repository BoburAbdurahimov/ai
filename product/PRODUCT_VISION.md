- Unlimited minutes

## ‚ö†Ô∏è Risks & Mitigations

### Risk 1: Low Adoption (AI distrust)
**Mitigation:**
- Free trial, easy to test
- Transparent pricing
- Video demos showing real calls
- Case studies from local businesses
- "Human fallback" as key feature

### Risk 2: High Churn
**Mitigation:**
- Onboarding excellence
- Proactive customer success
- Usage monitoring (flag low-usage customers)
- Quarterly business reviews
- Lock-in via integrations

### Risk 3: Competition
**Mitigation:**
- First-mover advantage in Uzbekistan
- Deep local integration
- Network effects (marketplace, integrations)
- Superior Russian/Uzbek AI quality
- Fast iteration based on local feedback

### Risk 4: Regulatory Changes
**Mitigation:**
- Legal counsel on retainer
- Call recording compliance
- Data sovereignty (Uzbekistan servers)
- Flexible architecture for policy changes
- Industry association membership

### Risk 5: Technical Scalability
**Mitigation:**
- Serverless architecture (auto-scaling)
- Multi-region from day one
- Load testing from MVP
- Monitoring and alerting
- Disaster recovery plan
