- Click waveform to jump to time
- Download as MP3
- Share link (24h expiry)

- Timestamped messages
- Speaker labels (AI vs Caller)
- Searchable
- Copy to clipboard
- Highlight keywords

- Confidence score (0-100%)
- Sentiment (positive/neutral/negative with emoji)
- Detected topics (tags)
- Intent classification
- Resolution status

**Tags:**
- Predefined tags (from dropdown)
- Custom tags
- Click tag to filter calls

**Notes:**
- Internal notes (not shown to customer)
- Rich text editor
- @mention team members
- Timestamps

**Actions:**
- Download (audio + transcript)
- Email (send to customer/team)
- Flag for review
- Delete (confirmation required)
